About Us

Mission

The Digital Studio serves students enrolled in any English course who wish to print course-related assignments, create digital projects, or work collaboratively. We offer additional services to students enrolled in ENGL 3764: Technical Writing, including online tutoring and on-site support for collaborative projects. We develop online content to assist Technical Writing students with assignments in this course with an emphasis on developing functional, critical, and rhetorical computer literacies. Digital Studio consultants aim to provide services with an equal blend of professionalism and creativity.

Meet the Team

Our team is comprised of dedicated graduate and undergraduate students from the English department. Our interns are all Professional and Technical Writing majors who are working hard to develop content for this website and provide online tutoring services for Technical Writing students. Keep reading to find out more about each of us!

Allison is a fourth-year PhD candidate in the Rhetoric and Writing program here at Virginia Tech.
